The Impact of Technology on the Workplace: How Technology is Changing

August 20, 2025

Eastern Europe has become a hub for top-notch software development companies that feature two distinctive features:

  • high-quality services;
  • competitive rates.

According to the research “IT Outsourcing Trends & Overview” which has been updated every year since 2019, Eastern Europe is the number one region to hire a development company. But why? And how to find expert development providers among thousands of companies? We will examine it in detail in this article as well as guide you on to find the most suitable = software development partner for your project.

What Makes the Eastern European Tech Scene Unique?

These countries share similar characteristics that set them apart from other countries. One of the most notable is the quality STEM education - the region produces many tech-savvy graduates every year, and their skills are among the highest on platforms for developers like LeetCode, HackerNoon, TopCoder, etc. These platforms have published rankings of countries based on programming skills, and Eastern Europe countries consistently rank high.

Moreover, Eastern European developers are widely recognized for their adaptability, strong communication skills, and client-oriented service approach. This moves us to the next point: high proficiency in English. IT teams from Eastern Europe can communicate and write technical documentation in English without language barriers, thanks to two facts: studying English is a part of the school and university curriculum, and there are many specialized language courses tailored to business and IT terminology.

Why Refer to Software Development in Eastern Europe

Eastern Europe has become well known as a hub for outsourcing software development, offering a blend of talented professionals, cost efficiency and shared cultural values. The region’s appeal is bolstered by an education system, a focus on innovation and research and a diverse group of developers skilled in cutting edge technologies.

Countries here offer an excellent balance between quality and cost efficiency. While developers in Eastern Europe command competitive rates compared to their Western counterparts, the quality of their work often exceeds expectations.

The country’s educational system produces a diverse talent pool, making it easy to find specialists in any programming language and framework. For instance, if you’re looking for a native mobile developer, there are senior professionals with over 10 years of experience in Java or Kotlin. Need a machine learning specialist? You’ll find coders proficient in Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, and other relevant technologies.

Another advantage is that Eastern Europe refers to the UTC+02:00 time zone, which is 2 hours ahead of Coordinated Universal Time. For global companies, this means software development companies can easily adjust their working schedule to reach the necessary number of overlapping hours.

Still, the most valued advantages of this region are:

  • Lower labor expenses compared to Western nations.

  • High quality of work.

  • Similar to US work ethics and values that enhance communication.

  • English language fluency among developers.

  • A high number of overlapping working hours with Clients in Europe and North America.
