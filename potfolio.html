Goals

  • Simplicity and Ease of Use: Design a clean, intuitive interface with straight forward navigation to ensure non-technical users can easily access all features.

  • Clear Lead Generation Process: Simplify the lead generation process with step-by-step guides and clear instructions to help users understand how to generate and verify leads.

  • Comprehensive Support and Resources: Offer accessible customer support, including FAQs, tutorials, and live chat, to assist users with any issues they encounter.

  • Payment Gateway Integration: Integrate payment gateways such as Stripe and Authorize.net to enable agents to easily take payments for marketing services during calls.

  • Mobile-Friendly Design: Ensure the website is fully responsive and functions seamlessly on all devices, including smartphones and tablets.

  • Visual Guidance: Use visuals, icons, and infographics to guide users through various processes, making the site more engaging and easier to understand.

Project Management Approach:

An Agile approach with incremental, milestone-based tasks was used.

Technology Stack:

Development:

  • Platform: PHP WordPress

  • Theme: Custom theme with ACF Flexible content based theme for easier content management

  • Frontend: SCSS for frontend frameworks.

DevOps:

The WordPress application is hosted on Cpanel hosting maintained and managed by diligent with automated code deployments from GitHub Actions.
